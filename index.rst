Welcome to Medtronic: Soft Robot For Surgical Interventions's documentation!
============================================================================
Medtronic: Soft Robot is a software that is used to control and collect data of a soft robot
developed by the team 7 of the 2021-2022 mechanical engineering senior design at
the University of Colorado Boulder.
